Interactive Visualization using D3 - Parallel Coordinates

Nia Vivek and Shannon Hamilton

This visualization displays data from the Times Higher Education for the top 30 universities

Select a year, click on a line for more information, move the axis around by clicking on the axis name and select a subset of data by dragging on the axis

Note: Null values for Income, Staff to Student Ratio, and International Score in the data were given the average value of that dimension.
Select year:
Radar Chart

KEY:

Total Score - total score for university, used to determine rank.
Teaching Score- university score for teaching (the learning environment).
Research Score- university score for research (volume, income and reputation).
Citation Score - university score for citations (research influence).
International Score - university score international outlook (staff, students, research).
Income Score - university score for industry income (knowledge transfer).
